Question 1: How do I forward calls on my Android phone?


Answer: To forward calls on your Android phone, open the Phone app and tap the three dots in the top-right corner. Then, tap "Settings" and select "Call forwarding." From there, you can choose to forward all calls, or you can choose to forward only certain types of calls, such as international calls or calls from specific phone numbers.

Question 2: Is there a cost to forwarding calls?


Answer: The cost of forwarding calls varies depending on your carrier. Some carriers offer free call forwarding, while others may charge a monthly fee or a per-minute rate. It is important to check with your carrier to find out the cost of call forwarding before you enable the feature.

Question 3: Can I forward calls to any number?


Answer: Yes, you can forward calls to any number, including landlines and mobile phones. However, it is important to note that some carriers may not allow you to forward calls to international numbers.

Question 4: How do I cancel call forwarding?


Answer: To cancel call forwarding, open the Phone app and tap the three dots in the top-right corner. Then, tap "Settings" and select "Call forwarding."
